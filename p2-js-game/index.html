Welcome to the world of magic and monsters!

In this game, you play as a ‘mangkukulam’ who has the power to cast spells and summon dark creatures through the use of cards.

Your goal is to reduce the enemy's life points to zero to emerge victorious.

Here's a quick guide on how to play:

GAMEPLAY

1. Both players start their turn with 100 life points and 5 energy points.

2. Each player is randomly assigned three cards each turn.

3. Attack your opponent by clicking on your cards.

4. Each card costs energy. Once you've used up your energy and cards, click 'End Turn' to pass the turn to your opponent.

5. Continue taking turns with your opponent until one of you reduces the other's life points to zero.

CARD TYPES

MALIGNO

These are creature cards that have an assigned attack value and energy cost. You can click a Maligno card to attack the enemy. The energy cost of each card varies, so make sure that you have enough energy before using them.

RANDOM

These cards reveal their true nature when clicked. Random cards cost 2 energy to cast. When you click a random card, you'll either get a Kulam, Sumpa, or Agimat card.

KULAM

Use this card to launch a magical attack against the enemy. Click a Kulam card to deal damage to the enemy.

SUMPA

Clicking a Sumpa card will decrease your life points. Be careful in clicking this card.

AGIMAT

This card adds a certain value to your life points. Clicking an Agimat card can save you in a pinch.
